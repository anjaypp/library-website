Rating:4/5

Author:Matthew McConaughey

Description: In this memoir, actor Matthew McConaughey reflects on his life through a collection of personal stories, insights, and life lessons. The title refers to the idea of "greenlights"—moments when life says "go," signaling that you are on the right path. McConaughey's unconventional wisdom and approach to life make this book an inspirational read​.
